How our product works

Customers have to scan the card which represents the desired item.
Item is added to the cart, data of the item will be revealed and touch screen will ask you quantity.
Choose it and those data will be saved in our system. Total amount and item list will be ready at the cashier.
Customer just have to check their bought items and take them easily.
