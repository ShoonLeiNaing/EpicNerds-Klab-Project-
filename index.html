Problems in Society

Since we are now facing the second wave of Covid-19 in Myanmar, people may be afraid to touch and buy things in supermarkets because virus can be attached on the groceries they bought.

Moreover, no one wants to push the heavy trolley and do shopping during the pandemic.


Customers may buy a lot of items (doing panic buying) . The more items we buy, the longer we have to wait at the counter for billing. This causes a long queue disobeying the rule of "Social Distancing".

Our Solutions

Reduce virus spreading.

Through our project, customers do not have to touch the products so that less chance for virus spreading.

No more heavy trolleys.

Customers do not have to push a heavy trolley if they buy a lot of items. No wonders, the younger people can move it easily but for the older people it will be quite difficult to move the heavy trolley.

Reduce long queues.

We do not have to wait at the cashier for calculating the total price since our system will automatically calculate the total bill as soon as the customer add or reduce items.
It will reduce long queues for payment.
